Recent Project

(November-December 2024)

This project took place in my Data Mining and General Linear Models (STAT172) course at Drake University. WesleyLife was looking to expand their Meals on Wheels program to other areas in Iowa. They tasked us to make reccomendations on different Public Use Microdat Areas (PUMA's) in Iowa based on verious food insecurity measures.

Using R, we trained differnt machine learning based models and used those to make predictions on food insecutiry rates by PUMA. We also used the models to run variance important tests to see which variables were most important in predicting food insecurity rates. We then used these results to make reccomendations to WesleyLife.
